Frequently Asked Questions

What databases does SQL Nurse support?+
SQL Nurse currently supports SQLite, MySQL, and PostgreSQL. We're continuously working to add support for more database systems.
Is my data secure?+
Yes, security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption for all data transfers and storage. Your database credentials are never stored in plain text.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?+
Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your paid features until the end of your billing period.
Do you offer team plans?+
Currently, we offer individual plans only. However, we're actively developing team collaboration features and expect to launch team plans in the near future.
How accurate is the natural language query generation?+
Our AI model has been trained on thousands of database schemas and queries, achieving over 90% accuracy for common query types. For complex queries, we provide the generated SQL for you to review and edit if needed.
